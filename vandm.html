Vision

As a student-run enterprise, our vision at Crispy Creations is to become the leading provider of nutritious and delicious Filipino snacks for parents and teachers in our community. We aim to create a sustainable business model that offers valuable learning opportunities and inspiration for student entrepreneurs on the campus of PISQ while meeting the changing needs and preferences of our customers. We strive to build a positive and lasting impact on the health and well-being of our community and to inspire student entrepreneurs to pursue their passions and create positive change in the world.

Mission

At Crispy Creations, our mission is to provide parents and teachers with high-quality and affordable lumpiang gulay and lumpiang shanghai made with fresh and locally sourced ingredients. We are committed to upholding the highest standards of quality, hygiene, and customer service in every aspect of our business, from preparation to packaging to delivery. As a student-run enterprise, we believe in the power of education, innovation, and entrepreneurship to create a positive social and economic impact. Our purpose is to establish a sustainable and profitable business that benefits not only our customers but also our team of student entrepreneurs and the wider community.
